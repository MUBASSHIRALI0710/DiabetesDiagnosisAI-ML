About Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body turns food into energy. There are two main types:

  • Type 1: The body does not produce insulin.
  • Type 2: The body does not use insulin properly (more common).

🚨 Common Risk Factors

  • Family history of diabetes
  • Overweight or obesity (high BMI)
  • Physical inactivity
  • High blood pressure
  • Age (45 years or older)
  • Gestational diabetes during pregnancy

📊 How This Tool Works

Our model uses a Support Vector Machine (SVM) trained on the Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset. It analyses 8 medical parameters to estimate your diabetes risk. The model achieves ~77% accuracy on test data.

⚠️ Disclaimer: This tool is for educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice.
